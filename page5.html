School Services Credentials


OSSE offers Standard Service Provider credentials for four years  and are renewable based upon satisfactory renewal requirements. Applicants must meet all the requirements for the standard credential. There is no initial or provisional type of credential available for Service Provider credentials.

School services include: School Audiologist, Reading Specialist, School Counselor, School Librarian, School Psychologist, School Social Worker, and School Speech Pathologist. 

Review both the indiviudal eligiblity requirements for each poistion and required documents needed to complete the application.

Offical Transcripts

Applicants must hold a bachelors and masters degree (if applicable.) Offical transcripts must be presented as confirmation.

Verification of program completion

Must verify completed an approved educator certification program in the area of specialization. See here. 

Test Scores

Applicants must submit all pages of examinee score report verifying passing scores for the DC-required school service provider exam OR comparable exam passed in another state where a valid license is held. 

Verification of past work experience

Verification of prior full-time experience form, verifying school based employment in the area of specialization. See here. 

Copy of DOH license (if applicable)

A copy of your valid license issued by the appropriate DC Department of Health (DOH) professional licensing board. Required if the heath-based area of specialization requires a DOH license to practice.

Out of state crediential (if applicable) 

A copy of your valid full school service provider credential, if held in another state. (only if applicable)
